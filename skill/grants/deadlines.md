## Cohort / cycle-based programs

### Colosseum
- **Model:** Cohort accelerator — applications open after each hackathon
- **Next hackathon:** Watch https://www.colosseum.org for announcements
- **Typical cadence:** 2–3 hackathons per year
- **Accelerator cohort:** Starts ~6–8 weeks after hackathon ends
- **Action:** If you placed in a recent hackathon, apply to the accelerator now

### Jupiter DAO (JUP grants)
- **Model:** Governance vote — proposals go to community vote
- **Voting cycle:** ~2 weeks per proposal
- **How to apply:** Post on https://vote.jup.ag — needs community discussion first
- **Tip:** Spend 1 week in the Jupiter Discord building support before posting a formal proposal. Cold proposals rarely pass.

### Superteam Regional Bounties
- **Model:** Rolling but region-specific deadlines
- **Check:** https://superteam.fun/bounties for active regional listings
- **Typical deadline:** 2–4 weeks from posting

→ **Superteam** — fastest decision, lowest friction

### If you can wait 2–3 months for a larger amount

## Application timing tips

- **Don't apply to more than 2 programs at once** — quality drops, and some programs ask if you're applying elsewhere
- **Solana Foundation + Superteam simultaneously is fine** — different scope, different amounts
- **Reapply window:** Most programs allow reapplication after 3 months if rejected
- **Best time to apply:** Early in the month — reviewers tend to clear queues at month start
- **Avoid December** — review cycles slow down significantly, decisions slip to January
